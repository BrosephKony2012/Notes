The Inquisition is an organization that is granted absolute authority to persecute all enemies of the imperium, be they within, or without. There are three branches of the Inquisition, the Ordo Hereticus, the Ordo Malleus, and the Ordo Xenos. 

- The Ordo Hereticus searches for and purges all heretical elements within the imperium, be they simple traitors, or the vile worshippers of Chaos.
- The Ordo Xenos hunts down Aliens, their conspirators, and their artefacts.
