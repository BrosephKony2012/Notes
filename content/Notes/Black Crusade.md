The party plays as either Human Cultists of Chaos, or Chaos Space Marines (Mixing the two is ill-advised so we will not be doing it).
The game focuses on walking the tightrope between pushing boundaries to acquire infamy, without pushing too hard and acquiring Corruption.

Pro's:
- The Infamy system gives you a real sense of progression as it impacts a variety of systems
- The Corruption system can throw a wrench into your character that lets you take them in a new direction you hadn't planned
- Lets you play an evil campaign without party strife (for the most part)
- Alignment based progression that lets you lead your character into the stereotype of their god

Cons:
- Fairly lethal at least early on (this also applies to enemies)
