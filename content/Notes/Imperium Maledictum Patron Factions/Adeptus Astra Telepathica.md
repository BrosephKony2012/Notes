
These guys manage psykers and shit like that, wherein you will either be working for an astropath, likely the leader of an Astropathic Choir, psykers soulbound to the Emperor on Terra that communicate across the void of space via telepathic imagery. The other option for a patron is a Knight-Centura of the Sisters of Silence, an organization that helps safeguard the Emperor as well as manage his Black Ships, hunting down psykers throughout the imperium and bringing them to Terra to either be soulbound, or fed to the Astropathic Choir of the Golden Throne, which projects the Astronomicon, a sort of Psychic north star that allows ships to navigate within the Warp.
